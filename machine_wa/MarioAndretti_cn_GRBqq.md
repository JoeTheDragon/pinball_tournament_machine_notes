### Physical Setup
-   Level Machine
-   Outlane Posts on "Hard Settings". I.e. most open.
-   Verify Tilt Sensitivity
-   Verify Volume
-   Ear Plug Tilt Hack (see photo)
-   Check coin door ball save.
-   Check Flipper Strength
