## Switch Setup
-   5-Ball Play
-   Extra Balls -- disable if possible (drop target Adjustment Plug)
-   Hi Score Adjustment Plug: make it lowest score possible, to replenish credits
-   Double Bonus Feature: 1,3,5 on a 5-ball game
