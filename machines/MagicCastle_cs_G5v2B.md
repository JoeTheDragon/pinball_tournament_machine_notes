### Standard Adjustments
-   11,13,15: Coin value 10
-   12,14,16: coin credits 15
-   19, Max credits: 30
-   20, Balls: 5 (for PinGolf) or 3 (for Round E and Rookie)
### Posted Notes
-   Bonus balls count as part of Ball 5
